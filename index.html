About the Center

Founded in 1996 by Rabbi Shalom Elmaleh z"l, Naar Yisrael is a Chabad House located in the heart of Milan's Center, right in front of the Sforzesco Castle, at Piazza Castello, 5. Naar Yisrael is open to all, regardless of affiliation, knowledge of Hebrew and level of commitment. Our Chabad House offers weekly Shabbat services and meals, unique Holidays (have you seen our Sukkah inside the Sforzesco Castle?) in a warm, joyous atmosphere. You'll feel comfortable and at home in this heimish enviroment.
